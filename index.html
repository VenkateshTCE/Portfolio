About me

Since 1994 onwards I am working as a teaching faculty in various positions as lecturer, Assistant professor, Associate professor , Professor and guiding undergraduate engineering students in Thiagarajar College of Engineering, Madurai, Tamilnadu, India.

Received BOYSCAST Fellowship scheme award in the year 2006 from Department of Science and Technology (DST), India for carrying out Post Doctoral research work at The Pennsylvania State University, University Park, USA.

Fellow in Institution of Engineers (India) (IE (India), Senior member in IEEE (USA) and a life member in Indian Society for Technical Eduaction (ISTE).
